OpenMP Best Practices

  • Race Conditions: Protect shared variables with critical sections, atomic operations, or locks
  • False Sharing: Align data to cache lines and add padding between thread-local data
  • Load Imbalance: Use dynamic or guided scheduling for uneven workloads
  • Synchronization: Minimize barriers and critical sections for better scalability
  • Memory: Be aware of NUMA effects and organize data for better locality
